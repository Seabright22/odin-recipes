Carbonara

Description

Roman pasta dish containing eggs, cheese and bacon, apparently created for stationed American soldiers.

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Curly Leaf Parsley
  • Pancetta
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Parmesan
  • Dry White Wine
  • Eggs

Method

  1. Bring a pot of mildly salted water to the boil, add the pasta and boil until cooked.
  2. Add the whole cloves of garlic a hot frying pan along with the olive oil.
  3. One golden, remove the cloves and add the pancetta (thinly sliced), and fry until crispy.
  4. Add a splash of wine, then cook until the wine is reduced before removing from the heat.
  5. Combine in a bowl the eggs, the grated cheeses, the chopped parsley and a liberal amount of black pepper.
  6. Just before the pasta is done, reheat the bacon, and then add the pasta and bacon straight from the pans into the mixing bowl.
  7. Serve.

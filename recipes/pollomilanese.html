Chicken Milanese

Description

A modification of the more tradional veal, this is an easy main meal to be paired with a side, such as a salad and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Flour
  • Butter (or neutral oil)
  • Parmesan
  • Eggs

Method

  1. Flatten the chicken to the height of a Â£1 coin.
  2. Dredge the chicken in a mixture of salt, pepper and flour, then the beaten eggs.
  3. Add the chicken to a mixture of parmesan and breadcrumbs.
  4. Melt the butter in a cast iron pan on a mid heat or neutral oil on a high.
  5. Add the breaded chicken until golden brown on the outside and cooked in the middle.
  6. Serve with choice of sides.

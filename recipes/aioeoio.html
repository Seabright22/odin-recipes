Aio e Oio

Description

A quick and easy pasta dish, originally from Napoli and modified for this roman variation.

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Curly Leaf Parsley
  • Red Chilli Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Method

  1. Bring a pot of heavily salted water to the boil, add the pasta and boil until cooked.
  2. Very thinly slice the garlic, and add to a hot frying pan along with the olive oil.
  3. Finely chop the chilli and to the frying pan once the garlic is golden until fragrant.
  4. Add the parsley and cook for a few seconds, then add the pasta straight from the boiling pan.
  5. Mix until combined, then serve.
